Ichigo's Best Fights IMO

Ichigo's battle with Ulquiorra is one of the best in the series and has a transformation that's top tier. It was the type of power-up that came a bit out of nowhere, but when it looks as cool as this one did, no one is going to complain. The interesting aspect is that once he takes on his new hollow form, his badge breaks, as shown in a few chapter sketches. It's an interesting tidbit, showing that Ichigo's shinigami side was no longer in control.

This was one of the most consequential fight scenes during the Soul Society arc, and it has a personal edge, too. Ichigo and his friends infiltrated the Soul Society to rescue Rukia from her cruel fate, and Renji made sure to stand in their way with his sword drawn.
